Our Story

WanderSmith is more than a travel guide; it's a journey crafted by passion, awe, and sheer love for discovery. WanderSmith was founded in 2025 by its creator, Kai Smith, and was created as a beacon of guidance for travelers to not only search for the most amazing places, but also to receive the quality of travel suggestions that make the ordinary trip extraordinary.

In a world increasingly dominated by over-saturated information and boundless options, I felt there was room for a reliable, customized travel guide. Born out of years of globe-trotting, WanderSmith was designed with one mandate in mind: to help individuals travel smarter, observe more, and, most importantly, make memories that last a lifetime.
